The Fellowship

We have something special here. Working at Airbnb is like strapping yourself to a rocket ship. The growth is exhilarating. The work is profoundly meaningful and rewarding. And, just when you’re gasping for oxygen, your colleagues are there to give you a hug and a high-five and re–energize you.

The Graphic Design team at Airbnb is a small, tightly knit team that serves as stewards of the company’s visual brand. We focus on creating meaningful offline experiences for Airbnb’s hosts, guests, and employees around the world—from producing top-notch marketing collateral to crafting beautiful icons for our office lunch menus. We eat together, brainstorm together, and play together. And as a team, we serve the creative needs of nearly every other department and office at Airbnb.

If you have a passion for taking open-ended problems and coming up with delightfully creative, concrete solutions—and you never take yourself too seriously—you’ll get along with us just fine. And you may want to consider becoming a Graphic Design Fellow.

The Airbnb Graphic Design Fellowship provides outstanding young designers to challenge themselves in an incredibly fast-paced, rigorous environment while receiving support, training, and mentorship from some of the best in the business. Fellows will explore new skill areas and understand the interaction between visual design and business. Throughout their six-month tenure, Fellows will build exceptional portfolios while being fully immersed in an iterative, holistic, thought-driven design process.

With the guidance and support of a designated mentor, Fellows will conceive, lead, and execute their own projects. They will work with and learn from the other Graphic Designers, as well as Design Researchers, User Experience Researchers, Interaction Designers, User Interface Designers, and a range of other departments. They’ll also be taught the exclusive Airbnb Graphic Designer handshake, once it has been created.

By the end of the five-month period, a Fellow may have had a hand in everything from externally facing print communication pieces to internal experiential work for the entire company. Past fellows have worked on a vintage inspired travel poster series, collateral for attracting top talent to Airbnb, internal team branding, environmental graphics, motion animations, and print communication for our community. Fellows are also able to proudly sign their name to one overarching project that will reflect their time and work at Airbnb.

If you don’t communicate extraordinarily well with others, hate taking initiative, and need to work on only one project at a time, Airbnb probably isn’t the place for you. But if you’re looking for a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment that will push you every day, it might be just the ticket.

You’ll be amazed at what you can achieve here.

The Basics:
  • Six months, full–time (40 hours per week)
  • Includes all office life perks (free gourmet meals, weekly happy hour, etc.)
  • Accommodations in Airbnb listings will be provided
  • Open to recent grads who have completed a BFA or MFA in Design in the past 18 months

Start Date: June 9, 2014
End Date: December 12, 2014

Tasks may include:
  • Fully concepting, leading, and executing projects
  • Active participation in design critiques and brainstorms
  • Design of offline communications pieces and online marketing pieces
  • Design of events materials and environmental graphics
  • Design of information graphics and presentations
  • Research inspiration, materials, merchandise and production methods
  • Research creative talent
  • Participating in and leading a cross–departmental skill sharing Design Camp session
