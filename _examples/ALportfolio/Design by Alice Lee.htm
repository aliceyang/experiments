About / JFDI

Curiouser and curiouser

Hi! I'm Alice, I'm a designer / illustrator. Right now I'm spending a lot of time drawing letters, illustrating stories, and exploring digital painting. I'm designing usually either in the offices of Dropbox HQ (where I'm a product designer and illustrator) or in a coffee shop while playing Adventure Time in the background, of course. A long time ago, a mentor told me: “if you don't know how to do something, just figure it out,” and it's been my personal approach to design ever since (previously, I was an undergraduate at the Wharton School studying business (lol)). These days, I also always have a side project - or two - going on personally. I'm just having a lot of fun exploring!

Want to get in touch? Let's get coffee (or pizza, I love that too).
